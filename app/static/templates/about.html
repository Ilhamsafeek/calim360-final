Our Story

CALIM 360 was born from a simple observation: contract management in the construction, oil & gas, and infrastructure sectors was fundamentally broken. Organizations were losing millions to disputes, claims, and compliance failures that could have been prevented with better tools and processes.

"We set out to build the contract management platform we wished existed—one that combines the precision of engineering with the intelligence of AI and the security of blockchain."

Our founding team brings together decades of experience in project management, legal advisory, software engineering, and enterprise systems. We've worked on mega-projects across the Middle East, managed complex dispute resolutions, and built enterprise software used by thousands of organizations.

Today, CALIM 360 serves leading organizations in Qatar and the broader GCC region, helping them manage billions of dollars in contracts with confidence. Our platform is specifically designed for the Qatar business environment, with built-in support for Qatar Civil Code compliance, QFCRA regulations, and Arabic language support.
