1.1 The Service Provider agrees to provide the following services ("Services"):

[Describe the specific services to be provided, including deliverables, timelines, and any specific requirements]

2. PAYMENT TERMS

2.1 In consideration for the Services, the Client agrees to pay the Service Provider a total fee of [AMOUNT] according to the following payment schedule:

  • 40% upon completion of Phase 1
  • 30% upon final delivery

2.2 All payments shall be made within 30 days of receipt of invoice.

3. CONFIDENTIALITY

3.1 Both parties acknowledge that they may have access to confidential information. Each party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all confidential information received from the other party.

4.1 This Agreement shall commence on [START DATE] and continue until [END DATE], unless terminated earlier in accordance with this Agreement.

4.2 Either party may terminate this Agreement upon 30 days written notice to the other party.
